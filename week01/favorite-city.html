My Favorite City

Abidjan, the economic capital of Côte d'Ivoire, is a vibrant and dynamic city located along the Ébrié Lagoon on the Atlantic coast. Often referred to as the "Paris of West Africa," it is known for its modern skyline, bustling markets, and rich cultural scene. As the country’s largest city, Abidjan serves as a hub for finance, commerce, and transportation in the region. Neighborhoods like Plateau, Cocody, and Treichville each offer a unique glimpse into the city's diversity, from business centers and universities to lively music and nightlife. With its mix of traditional Ivorian culture and contemporary urban life, Abidjan stands out as a symbol of resilience and growth in West Africa.
